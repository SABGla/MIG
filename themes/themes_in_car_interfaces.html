In-car Interfaces

A lot of our recent research has investigated interfaces for drivers and passengers in cars.

Recent Projects

  • HAPPINESS: in-car haptic interfaces
  • Multimodal warnings for drivers
  • In-car virtual reality headset use
  • Thermal in-car haptic interaction

Collaborations

Recent automotive interaction projects in the group have been funded by Freescale Semiconductor and Jaguar-Land Rover. We have also worked with companies such as Bosch through the EU-funded HAPPINESS project.
