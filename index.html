Who are we?

We are the Multimodal Interaction Group, led by Professor Stephen Brewster. We are part of the Glasgow Interactive Systems Section at the University of Glasgow. Read on for more information about our research into multimodal human-computer interaction. Our research covers audio and haptic feedback, gesture and pressure input, accessibility, mobility, in-car and in-home interfaces, and novel interaction techniques.
